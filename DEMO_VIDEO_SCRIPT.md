# Scrap Management System — Demo Video Script

**Duration:** ~3-4 minutes
**Tool:** Loom (loom.com) — install browser extension, click Record, choose "Screen + Cam"
**Resolution:** 1920×1080 (full screen)
**Tip:** Close all other tabs/notifications. Use a clean browser profile.

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Log in as **owner** account (so you can show all features)
- [ ] Make sure you have at least: 2 vendors, 2 buyers, 3 categories, 2 godowns, 1 truck
- [ ] Have 1-2 sample purchases and 1 sale already recorded (for ledger demo)
- [ ] Set Cash/Bank/UPI opening balances in Settings
- [ ] Open https://scrapemanagement-v2.onrender.com in a clean browser window

---

## SCENE 1 — Intro (0:00–0:15)

**Action:** Show the login page

**Voiceover:**
> "Meet **Scrap Management System** — a complete double-entry accounting and inventory app built for scrap dealers. Let me show you what it can do."

**On-screen:** Click "Sign In" with owner credentials

---

## SCENE 2 — Dashboard Overview (0:15–0:40)

**Action:** Land on Dashboard

**Voiceover:**
> "Here's the dashboard. Real-time stats: today's purchases, sales, outstanding amounts owed to vendors and from buyers, and current inventory value. Charts show recent trends at a glance."

**On-screen:**
- Hover over stat cards
- Scroll to charts
- Point at outstanding amounts

---

## SCENE 3 — Master Data (0:40–1:00)

**Action:** Click through Vendors → Buyers → Categories → Godowns → Trucks

**Voiceover:**
> "All your master data lives here — vendors, buyers, scrap categories, godowns, and trucks. Each entity tracks its own balance, contact info, and history."

**On-screen:** Spend ~3 seconds on each page

---

## SCENE 4 — Record a Purchase (1:00–1:30)

**Action:** Go to Purchases → Click "New Purchase"

**Voiceover:**
> "Recording a purchase is a single form. Pick the vendor, add items by category, weight, and rate. The system auto-calculates totals, creates ledger entries on both sides, and updates inventory immediately."

**On-screen:**
- Fill the form quickly with sample data
- Submit
- Show the new entry in the table

---

## SCENE 5 — Inventory & Stock (1:30–1:50)

**Action:** Go to Inventory page

**Voiceover:**
> "Inventory is godown-wise — you can see exactly how much of each category is in each godown. Stock updates automatically with every purchase, sale, and challan."

**On-screen:** Hover over stock rows

---

## SCENE 6 — Sales & Challans (1:50–2:15)

**Action:** Show Sales page, then Challans

**Voiceover:**
> "Sales work the same way — pick a buyer, choose what you're selling, and the inventory deducts automatically. For inter-godown stock movements, use Challans to track trucks moving material between locations."

---

## SCENE 7 — Payments & Double-Entry Ledger (2:15–2:50)

**Action:** Go to Payments → record a payment → then Ledger

**Voiceover:**
> "Record vendor payments or buyer collections in one click — cash, bank, or UPI. Behind the scenes, every transaction creates double-entry ledger records. Open the Ledger and you'll see master ledger, vendor ledger, and buyer ledger views with running balances."

**On-screen:**
- Quickly record a small payment
- Switch to Ledger
- Show vendor ledger with running balance
- **Click "Export to Excel" — show the file downloads**

---

## SCENE 8 — Expenses (2:50–3:10)

**Action:** Go to Expenses

**Voiceover:**
> "Track business expenses with custom categories — fuel, salary, rent, anything. Each expense automatically debits the expense account and credits cash, bank, or UPI."

**On-screen:** Add a quick expense, show it in the list

---

## SCENE 9 — Settings & Users (Owner-only) (3:10–3:30)

**Action:** Click Settings → then Users

**Voiceover:**
> "As the owner, you control everything — set opening balances for cash, bank, and UPI accounts, and manage your team in the Users section. Create managers, reset passwords, or deactivate accounts."

---

## SCENE 10 — Outro (3:30–3:50)

**Action:** Back to Dashboard

**Voiceover:**
> "Mobile-friendly, dark-mode ready, and built on modern web tech. Scrap Management System — proper accounting for the scrap business. Try it at **scrapemanagement-v2.onrender.com**."

**On-screen:** End on dashboard with the URL visible

## Recording Tips

1. **Practice once** without recording — gets the timing right
2. **Speak slowly** — viewers need time to read screens
3. **Pause briefly** between scenes — easier to edit later
4. **Use cursor highlight** in Loom settings — viewers can follow your clicks
5. **Re-record any scene** if you stumble — Loom lets you trim
6. After recording, Loom auto-uploads and gives a shareable link

Total recording time including retakes: ~30 minutes
